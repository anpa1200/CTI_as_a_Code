## Claim Format

```

- **Confidence**: High / Medium / Low  
  (High = multiple independent corroborating sources; Medium = single source, consistent; Low = inferred, no direct evidence)
- **Supporting evidence**: EV-XXX, EV-XXX
- **Reasoning**: [Brief explanation of how evidence supports claim]
- **Alternative explanations considered**: [What else could explain the evidence?]
- **PIR addressed**: PIR-XXX
```
