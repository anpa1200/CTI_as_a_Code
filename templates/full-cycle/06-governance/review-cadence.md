## Review Schedule

| Review Type | Frequency | Participants | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| PIR health check | Monthly | CTI team | Updated PIR statuses, collection adjustments |
| Detection review | Monthly | CTI + SOC | Tuned rules, backlog updates |
| Stakeholder briefing | Quarterly | CTI + CISO + SOC Lead | Updated requirements, feedback |
| Program metrics review | Quarterly | CTI team + CISO | Metrics report, roadmap update |
| Full program review | Annual | CTI + CISO + Legal + Compliance | Program charter renewal, regulatory compliance |
| MOU renewal check | Annual | CTI + Legal | MOUs renewed or renegotiated |

---

## Monthly Review Agenda

1. PIR status: any new triggers? Any PIRs needing update?
2. Detection: any rules firing? Any new gaps identified?
3. Collection: any source changes? New CERT-IL advisories?
4. Team capacity: upcoming leaves, blockers?
5. Action items from last month: all closed?

---

## Quarterly Review Agenda

1. Metrics review: production, detection, sharing, stakeholder satisfaction
2. PIR review: retire/add/update PIRs based on stakeholder needs
3. Roadmap review: on track? Blockers?
4. Threat landscape update: has the threat picture changed?
5. Stakeholder feedback incorporation
6. Budget and resource planning for next quarter

---

## Annual Review Agenda

1. Full program health assessment
2. Charter review and renewal
3. Regulatory compliance evidence review
4. MOU status: all active, renewal needed?
5. Team capability assessment: training gaps?
6. Technology review: are our tools still fit for purpose?
7. Lesson learned log review
8. Next year objectives setting
