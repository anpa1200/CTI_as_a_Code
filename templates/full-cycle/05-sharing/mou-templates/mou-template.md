## Purpose

This MOU establishes the framework for reciprocal sharing of cyber threat intelligence between Party A and Party B for the purpose of improving defensive capabilities and situational awareness for both organizations.

## Scope of Sharing

| Category | Party A Shares | Party B Shares |
|---|---|---|
| IOCs (IP, domain, hash) | Yes | Yes |
| Incident summaries | Sanitized | Sanitized |
| TTP analysis | Yes | Yes |
| Classified intelligence | No | No |

---

## Classification and Handling

- All shared intelligence is classified TLP:AMBER unless otherwise marked.
- Neither party will share received intelligence beyond TLP designation without explicit written consent.
- PII must be removed before sharing.

---

## Obligations

**Both parties agree to:**
- Share intelligence within [24 hours / 72 hours] of determining it has value to the other party.
- Respond to intelligence-sharing requests within [5 business days].
- Not attribute shared intelligence to the other party without written consent.
- Notify the other party immediately if shared intelligence is exposed or mishandled.
