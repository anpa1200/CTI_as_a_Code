## Section 7: Citizen Notification Assessment

Under Biometric Database Law Section 18(e), citizens whose biometric data was accessed
without authorization must be notified if:
- The access could enable identity theft or fraud
- The access included biometric template data
- BDA determines notification is in the public interest
