# MOU Templates

This directory holds MOU (Memorandum of Understanding) templates for NDSA intelligence sharing partnerships.

**Active MOU negotiations:**
- CERT-IL MOU — draft terms in `../sharing-architecture.md`; pending legal review
- ITA (Israel Tax Authority) MOU — framework in `../sharing-architecture.md`; not yet initiated

**INCD-specific MOU note:** Intelligence sharing with INCD is governed by INCD-CID regulations, not a bilateral MOU. The CERT-IL and ITA MOUs are for horizontal peer-sharing, distinct from the vertical INCD reporting obligation.

**Template:** See `03-full-cycle-techpay/05-sharing/mou-templates/README.md` for the standard bilateral MOU template. NDSA-specific additions: add INCD pre-notification clause and GOVNET classified exclusion clause in Section 2.
